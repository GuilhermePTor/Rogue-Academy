MODULE_00 // MISSION_BRIEF

Overview

This guide exists because the gap between training a model and running it reliably in production is where most engineers struggle. Books teach you to build the algorithm. Production teaches you data drift, latency budgets, feature stores, retraining pipelines, evaluation frameworks, and what happens at 3am when your model silently degrades. Every resource here is ranked by one criterion: does it teach you what actually breaks in real systems? The free track is comprehensive. Paid is optional depth.
